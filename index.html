Using CamoStudio with LucidSight

Step-by-Step Setup Guide:

  1. Download and install CamoStudio on your mobile device from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Launch CamoStudio and follow the in-app setup instructions
  3. Connect your mobile device to the same network as your computer
  4. On your computer, open LucidSight and select "Mobile Camera" as the video source
  5. Position your mobile device for optimal coverage of the area you want to monitor

Tip: For best results, place your mobile device on a stable surface or tripod and ensure good lighting.
